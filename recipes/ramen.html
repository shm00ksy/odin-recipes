Ramen

Description

Quick and easy. Instant ramen is filling, cheap, and delicious. You can make it as simple or complex as you'd like

Ingredients

  • 1 package instant ramen
  • 500ml water
  • Optional
    • 1 egg
    • green onion

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil
  2. Boil dry noodles for 3 minutes or until soft
  3. Optional
      4. At this point you can remove noodles to bowl and poach an egg or simmer green onions in water
  4. Add seasoning packet to bowl, pour noodles and water in bowl
  5. Eat!
