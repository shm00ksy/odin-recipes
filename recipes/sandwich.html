Sandwich

Description

As simple as it gets, yet packing flavor and nutrition

Ingredients

  • Your preffered bread
  • Sliced deli meat
  • Sliced cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Optional
    • Spinach
    • Lettuce
    • Tomatoes
    • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise on one slice of bread, mustard on the other slice
  2. Layer deli meats onto bottom slice
  3. Add cheese on top of meats
  4. Optional
    • Add your vegetables at this stage
  5. Add top slice to close it up
  6. Eat!
